About Jansen Library

Our Mission

At Jansen Library, The Learning Resource Center (LRC) is committed to providing quality and relevant information resources and services that will promote excellence in an academic community and educational research in the community and the Caraga region.

Our Vision

The Learning Resource Center is envisioned to be proactive in support of learning, teaching, and research of the university. A library manned by professional experts and highly competent staff with the latest collections of books, multimedia, and online resources.

What We Offer

  • Over 16,750 books in our physical collection
  • Digital resources and e-books
  • Quiet study areas and reading rooms
  • Reference services and research assistance
